Help/FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a book?

To rent a book, simply browse our collection, select the book you want, and click the "Rent Now" button. You’ll be prompted to log in or create an account if you don’t have one.

How long can I keep a rented book?

The rental duration is typically 30 days, but you can check specific terms for each book. Extensions may be available for an additional fee.

Are there late fees for overdue books?

Yes, a small late fee will apply for books returned after the due date. We recommend returning books on time to avoid additional charges.

How can I return a rented book?

You can return a book by visiting our "Return Book" section on the website. Follow the instructions provided, or visit our nearest drop-off location.

Can I rent audiobooks?

Yes, we offer a wide range of audiobooks. Browse our "Audiobooks" section to explore available titles and enjoy listening on the go.

What should I do if a book is damaged?

Please report any damage immediately through our "Contact Us" page. Fees may apply for significant damage beyond normal wear and tear.

How can I contact customer support?

For assistance, you can email us at support@rentaread.com or visit our "Contact Us" page.
