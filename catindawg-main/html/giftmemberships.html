A Rent-A-Read gift membership is the perfect present for any book lover. With access to thousands of books, audiobooks, and more, your loved ones can enjoy a new adventure every month.

  • Flexible membership plans to suit any reader's needs

  • Gift cards available for all membership tiers

How It Works

Giving a Rent-A-Read gift membership is simple! Follow these easy steps:

  1. Choose the membership plan you'd like to gift.
  2. Fill out the recipient's information and your payment details.
  3. Complete the order and send the gift membership via email or physical card.
  4. The recipient can redeem their gift and start enjoying their rentals right away!
